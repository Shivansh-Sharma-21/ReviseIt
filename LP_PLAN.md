# ReviseIt Landing Page Implementation Plan

## 1. Hero: The Problem-Solver
- **Headline**: "Diagnose Your Weaknesses. Protect Your Rank."
- **Sub-headline**: "ReviseIt is a targeted revision and diagnosis tool for JEE aspirants. Identify the concepts costing you marks due to false confidence, and fix them through adaptive practice."
- **Visual**: A split-screen or overlay showing "Typical DPP (Random Chaos)" vs "ReviseIt (Structured Flow & Diagnosis)".

## 2. Section: The "False Confidence" Trap
- **Concept**: Interactive element showing how a student might feel 100% confident in a topic but fail 'The Reality Check'.
- **Copy**: "Many aspirants lose out on thousands of ranks due to negative marking and false confidence. ReviseIt exposes the gaps you didn't know you had by diagnosing your baseline before you dive into advanced problems."

## 3. Section: Our 4-Step Revision Philosophy
A horizontal/vertical scroll section using the existing component aesthetics:
1. **Visualize**: Map out concepts through mindmaps (The XYFlow integration).
2. **Diagnose**: Take the mandatory baseline diagnostic (The Reality Check) to test core logic.
3. **Calibrate**: The app adapts question difficulty based on the gap between your honest self-assessment and actual diagnostic score.
4. **Master**: 15 targeted questions where foundation questions never truly disappear, ensuring your fundamentals lock in and your rank potential improves.

## 4. Key Pillars (Feature Grid)
- **Precision Diagnosis**: Find the delusion gap between what you think you know and what you actually know.
- **Zero Decision Fatigue**: No choosing what to do next. Just select the subject and follow the diagnostic flow.
- **Adaptive Intelligence**: Question tags (F/C/S) that morph strictly matched to your rank-improvement needs.

## 5. Final CTA: Protect Your Rank Today
- **Visual**: A premium subject selection card for Physics.
- **Button**: "Start Your Diagnosis"
