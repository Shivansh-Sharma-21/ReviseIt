1. **Visualize**: See the concepts through interactive mindmaps (The XYFlow integration).
2. **Expose**: The mandatory Foundation Checkpoint to test core logic.
3. **Calibrate**: Adjusting difficulty based on your honest self-assessment.
4. **Master**: 15 targeted questions where Foundation questions never truly disappear.

- **Anti-Distraction UI**: No leaderboards, no streaks, no fluff. Just physics.
- **Adaptive Intelligence**: Question tags (F/C/S) that morph to your session performance.
